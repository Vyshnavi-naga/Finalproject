Types of Makeup

Foundation

Foundation is used to create an even skin tone.

Eyeshadow

Eyeshadow adds color and depth to the eyelids.

Lipstick

Lipstick enhances the lips with color and shine.

Blush

Blush adds color to the cheeks for a healthy look.

Makeup Tips

  • Always apply moisturizer before foundation.
  • Use primer for a smooth base.
  • Blend eyeshadow well for a seamless look.
  • Choose lipstick shades that complement your skin tone.
  • Don't forget to set your makeup with powder.
