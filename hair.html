Loose Waves

Loose waves give a relaxed and effortless look.

  • Use a heat protectant before styling.
  • Try using a curling wand for defined waves.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.
High Ponytail

A high ponytail is sleek and perfect for any occasion.

  • Use a brush to gather your hair at the crown.
  • Secure tightly with a strong hair tie.
  • Wrap a section of hair around the base to hide the tie.
